== Description ==
Siteguard Security plugin logs and displays failed login attempt, errors and exceptions and provides detailed information to help you to supervise what happens to your site.

This plugin helps you monitor your site to have knowledge of what happens to your site.
If you are aware about what happens to your site, you can take the proper actions to avoid security problems and, most important, you can verify whether this actions are effective.
